About Me

My name is Sigrún Lárusdóttir and I am a graduated fashion designer from Iceland University of the Arts. I currently study Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA, Copenhagen. For the past years my work has been focused on interdisciplinary aspects of design, art and technology, and currently on digital Multimedia and journalism. I have worked and studied in Reykjavik, London, Paris and currently in Copenhagen.
